What makes a good website?

A Strategic Approach

A strategic approach ensures your website reflects your brand's values and mission, instantly enhancing credibility. Aligning design and content with your goals makes visitors recognize your professionalism.

Great—Not Good—Copywriting

High-quality copywriting captures your brand’s essence and speaks directly to your audience. Well-crafted content informs and persuades, converting visitors into loyal customers.

A Professional, Custom Design

Creating a professional, custom design combines creativity and technical skill for a standout, user-friendly website. This ensures visual appeal and brand alignment.

Effective Service Presentation

It's essential to help visitors grasp the full value of your offerings. Clear communication of services turns visitors into trusting clients.

A Scalable Platform

A scalable platform grows with your business, supporting future marketing initiatives seamlessly. This flexibility ensures your site can adapt to new content and features, keeping your audience engaged.

Cross-Device Compatibility

A mobile-friendly website ensures great functionality and appearance on all devices. This boosts engagement, allowing visitors to connect with your brand anytime, anywhere.

So, what's our game plan?

Web Design

A Professional Website that Aligns with RPSC's Brand

  • Implement a clean, mobile-friendly, modern design with high-quality images and professional layouts
  • Include an "About" section that highlights the organization's mission, vision, and the stories of its founders
  • Introduce key team members with their credentials and experiences in a "Team" section, and incorporate testimonials and case studies to build trust with potential clients and partners

Content Creation

Effectively present RPSC's three pillar services

  • Create dedicated pages for each service with detailed descriptions, benefits, and examples of previous work or case studies
  • Utilize visual elements such as icons, infographics, and relevant images to enhance the understanding of each service
  • Include clear calls to action to guide visitors to contact RPSC for more information or to engage their services

Future-Proofing

Develop a platform that supports future marketing initiatives

  • Build the website on a robust CMS like WordPress, allowing RPSC to easily add and manage content and new functionality
  • Include a blog section for sharing insights, news, and updates related to DEI, social impact, and RPSC’s activities
  • Integrate social media and email marketing tools to facilitate content distribution and audience engagement

Recommended Project Scope and Deliverables

  • Homepage design showcasing RPSC's credibility and services
  • Three service pages, each detailing one pillar service
  • About Us page to build trust with potential clients
  • Contact page with form for inquiries
  • Blog/Resources section for future content marketing
  • Scalability for adding guides, blog posts, white papers, etc.
  • Responsive design for all devices
  • SEO-friendly structure and initial optimization
  • Content management system setup and training

The Design & Development Process

Week One

Discovery

We'll dive deep into understanding Rock Paper Scissors Collective's mission and goals through in-depth interviews with key stakeholders. We'll analyze existing branding materials and research the target audience to ensure we capture the essence of RPSC. This phase sets the foundation for a website that truly reflects RPSC's unique value and vision.

Weeks Two + Three

Design & Content

We'll create wireframes and high-fidelity mockups to visualize the site's structure and aesthetic, ensuring everything aligns with RPSC's brand. We'll collaborate with RPSC to craft compelling copy that effectively communicates their mission and services. This phase transforms ideas into a tangible and engaging design ready for development.

Weeks Four + Five

Development & Launch

We'll bring the design to life on a robust CMS, ensuring the site is responsive and user-friendly. We'll conduct thorough testing to ensure everything works perfectly before the official launch. After the successful launch, we'll hand over the website with comprehensive training and documentation, empowering RPSC to manage their new online presence confidently.
